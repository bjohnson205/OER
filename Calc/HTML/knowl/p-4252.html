For each of the following functions, use your work in (a) to help you determine the general antiderivative 3  of the function. Label each antiderivative by name (e.g., the antiderivative of \(m\) should be called \(M\)). In addition, check your work by computing the derivative of each proposed antiderivative.

  1. \(m(x) = e^{3x}\)

  2. \(n(x) = \cos(5x+1)\)

  3. \(s(x) = \frac{1}{1+4x^2}\)

  4. \(v(x) = (2-7x)^3\)

  5. \(w(x) = 3^{4-11x}\)
