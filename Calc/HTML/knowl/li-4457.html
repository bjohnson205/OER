Item7.6.5.d

Since \(p(t)\) tells us the percentage of people who've heard the rumor, we need to solve the equation \(p(t) = 0.8\text{.}\) Thus we have

\begin{equation*} 0.8 = \frac{1}{9e^{-0.2t} + 1} \end{equation*}

so

\begin{equation*} 9e^{-0.2t} + 1 = 1.2 \end{equation*}

and \(e^{-0.2t} = 0.2/9\text{.}\) It follows that \(t = -5 \ln(0.2/9) \approx 19.033\) days.
