Item5.2.5.a

Since the tide removes sand at a rate of \(R(t) = 2 + 5\sin \left( \frac{4\pi t}{25} \right)\) cubic yards of sand per hour, on the time interval \([0,6]\text{,}\) it follows that since the units on \(R(t) \cdot \triangle t\) are ``cubic yards'', the total sand removed on this time interval is

\begin{equation*} \int_0^6 \left[2 + 5\sin \left( \frac{4\pi t}{25} \right) \right] \, dt\text{.} \end{equation*}
