Where \(x=0\text{,}\) we can see that \(y=-2\) and thus \((0,-2)\) is an ordered pair solution. This is the point where the graph intersects the \(y\)-axis and is called the \(y\)-intercept. We can use this point and the slope as a means to quickly graph a line.
