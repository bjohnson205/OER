\begin{equation*} m = \frac{\Delta y}{\Delta x}=\frac{4}{3} \end{equation*}
so starting at \((0, -2)\text{,}\) we move \(4\) units in the \(y\)-direction and \(3\) units in the \(x\)-direction, to arrive at the point \((3, 2)\text{.}\) Finally, we draw the line through these two points. (See FigureÂ 86.)
