\begin{equation*} \begin{aligned} 7x(3x-2)-(3x-2)\amp = 7x\alert{(3x-2)}-1\alert{(3x-2)}\\ \amp = \alert{(3x-2)}(\, ?\, )\\ \amp =(3x-2)(7x-1). \end{aligned} \end{equation*}

Factoring results in \(7x(3x-2)-(3x-2)=(3x-2)(7x-1)\text{.}\)
