The children in Francine’s art class are going to make cardboard boxes. Each child is given a sheet of cardboard that measures 18 inches by 24 inches. To make a box, the child will cut out a square from each corner and turn up the edges, as shown in Figure 135.

  1. Write a formula \(V = f (x)\) for the volume of the box in terms of \(x\text{,}\) the side of the cut-out square.
  2. What is the domain of the function? (What are the largest and smallest possible values of \(x\text{?}\))
  3. Graph the function and estimate its range.
