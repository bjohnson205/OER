Recall that in section \(3.4 \) we found that with a principal \(P \text{,}\) and interest rate \(r\text{,}\) if we compound this interest \(n\)-times a year the value, \(A(t)\text{,}\) of the investment after \(t\) years is given by the formula

\begin{equation*} A(t)=P(1+\dfrac{r}{n})^{nt}. \end{equation*}
