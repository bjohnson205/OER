However, it is very important to think about compatability of two functions before we start combining them in various ways. For example, to combine the functions \(f(x)\) and \(g(x) \) as

\begin{equation*} \frac{f(x)}{g(x)} \end{equation*}

we have to know that \(g(x)\neq 0 \) as we cannot divide by 0.
