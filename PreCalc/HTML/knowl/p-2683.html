We can start by finding two initial solutions that we encountered in the last chapter. These initial solutions are two common angles between 0 and \(2\pi\) on the unit circle with a cosine value of \(-\sqrt{2}/2\text{:}\)

\begin{equation*} \theta=\frac{3\pi}{4} \hspace{.25in} \text{and} \hspace{.25in} \theta=\frac{5\pi}{4} \end{equation*}
